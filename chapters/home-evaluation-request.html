The Santa Barbara County Fire Safe Council can help you and your family prepare for wildfires.

Click here for your free home hardening and defensible evaluation

A Wildfire Preparedness Evaluation is a free and voluntary review of the vulnerability of your house and landscape to wildfire and ember ignition. During this assessment, an evaluator will:

  • review defensible space and home hardening principles
  • make specific recommendations based on your property
  • review your evacuation preparedness so you can be safer and feel more prepared

Certain jurisdictions have Wildfire Specialists with the local fire department who also provide free home evaluations:

  • City of Santa Barbara
  • Montecito- contact Wildland Specialist Maeve Juarez: 805-565-8018 or mjuarez@montecitofire.com
  • Carpinteria/Summerland- (805) 684-4591
